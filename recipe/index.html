Yummy Nana-Nut Bread

Bananas aren't my favorite by themselves, but when you turn them into bread, something magical happens.

Photo creds: Nourish Plate

Here's what you need:

  • 3 old nanas
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 3/4 cups of flower
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • Lots of walnuts! (NOT OPTIONAL haha jk)
  • Cinnamon eyeball (to taste)
  • Nutmeg eyeball (optional)

Get ta cookin!

  1. Set the oven to 350Â°
  2. Mash your naners well
  3. Add your beaten eggs
  4. Add the olive oil
  5. Add vanilla
  6. Add the salt, cinnamon, and other spices
  7. Add baking soda
  8. Add walnuts
  9. Add flour and mix well
  10. Grease your bread pan (or muffin pan) well with olive oil and pour mixture. You can sprinkle some walnuts on top for some extra razzle dazzle.
  11. Bake for about 30 minutes, then check with a toothpick. Muffin pans are usually done, but bread pans may need an extra 5-10 minutes.
  12. Let them cool, then gobble them down! (Share if you can)
